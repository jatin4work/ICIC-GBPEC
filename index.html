Welcome to the official website of the International Conference on Intelligent Computing (ICIC'23)! We are delighted to present a platform dedicated to sharing groundbreaking research papers in the field of intelligent computing.

Organized by the esteemed Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at G.B. Pant DSEU Okhla-1 Campus, New Delhi (formerly GBPEC), in collaboration with the Government of NCT of Delhi, this conference is set to take place from the 15th to the 17th of February, 2024. ICIC'23 brings together leading researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and students from around the globe to exchange ideas and insights on cutting-edge advancements in the field of intelligent computing. It serves as a nexus for fostering collaborations, initiating discussions, and propelling the field forward.

This website serves as a hub for researchers to submit and explore the latest research papers in various areas of intelligent computing, including but not limited to artificial intelligence, machine learning, data science, robotics, and computer vision. By providing a platform for researchers to showcase their work, we aim to facilitate a vibrant intellectual discourse and promote innovation. Whether you are an established researcher or an aspiring scholar, this platform offers a unique opportunity to share your findings, gain visibility, and receive valuable feedback from the global scientific community. With the support of our esteemed organizing committee and the dedication of our participants, ICIC'23 promises to be an intellectually stimulating and enriching event.
